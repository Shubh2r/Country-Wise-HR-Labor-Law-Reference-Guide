**Country Name: Panama**

**🇵🇦 Panama – HR & Labour Law Guide (2026)**

**1\. Country Overview**

*   Primary Laws:
    *   _Labour Code of Panama (Código de Trabajo)_ – main employment law.
    *   _Social Security Law_.
    *   _Occupational Safety and Health Regulations_.
*   Regulatory Authority: Ministry of Labour and Labour Development (MITRADEL).
*   Applicability: Covers private sector employees; public sector governed separately.
*   Recent Developments (2025–26):
*   Adjustments to minimum wage by sector and region.
*   Strengthened occupational safety standards.
*   Expanded digital payroll and compliance systems.

**2\. Employment Contracts**

*   Types of Employment: Permanent, fixed‑term, temporary, apprenticeship.
*   Probationary Periods: Up to 3 months.
*   Mandatory Clauses: wages, hours, leave, termination conditions.
*   Language Requirement: Spanish mandatory.
*   Written Contracts: Required for all employees.

**3\. Working Hours & Overtime**

*   Standard Hours: 8 hours/day, 48 hours/week.
*   Overtime Rules:
    *   _1.25×_ hourly wage (daytime).
    *   _1.5×_ (night work).
    *   _2×_ (public holidays).
*   Rest Periods: Weekly rest day (Sunday).

**4\. Leave & Holidays**

*   Annual Leave: 30 calendar days/year (after 11 continuous months of service).
*   Public Holidays: ~12 annually (including Independence Days, Christmas, New Year, Carnival).
*   Sick Leave: Paid via social security (medical certificate required).
*   Maternity Leave: 14 weeks (paid).
*   Paternity Leave: 3 days (paid).

**5\. Compensation & Benefits**

*   Minimum Wage: Varies by sector and region (~USD 326–970/month depending on industry, updated 2025).
*   Mandatory Contributions:
    *   Social Security Fund: employer ~12%, employee ~9.75%.
    *   Education tax: employer ~1.5%.
*   Bonus/13th Month Pay: Mandatory “Décimo Tercer Mes” (13th month bonus, paid in three installments annually).

**6\. Termination & Severance**

*   Notice Periods:
    *   30 days (unless payment in lieu).
*   Severance Pay:
    *   3.4 weeks’ wages per year of service (capped).
*   Exemptions: No severance if dismissed for misconduct.

**7\. Employee Obligations**

*   Must comply with company rules and statutory obligations.
*   Must give contractual notice for resignation (usually 30 days).
*   Employees must contribute to social security.

**8\. Compliance & Enforcement**

*   MITRADEL: Oversees compliance.
*   Labour Courts: Handle disputes.
*   CBAs: Binding and enforceable across industries.

**9\. References**

*   Ministry of Labour and Labour Development (MITRADEL) – official portal.
