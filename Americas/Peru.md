**Country Name: Peru**

**🇵🇪 Peru – HR & Labour Law Guide (2026)**

**1\. Country Overview**

*   Primary Laws:
    *   _Labour Productivity and Competitiveness Law (LPCL)_ – contracts, termination.
    *   _General Labour Law_.
    *   _Occupational Safety and Health Law_.
    *   _Social Security Law_.
*   Regulatory Authority: Ministry of Labour and Employment Promotion (MTPE).
*   Applicability: Covers private sector employees; public sector governed separately.
*   Recent Developments (2025–26):
*   Strengthened occupational safety standards.
*   Adjustments to minimum wage.
*   Expanded digital payroll and compliance systems.

**2\. Employment Contracts**

*   Types of Employment: Permanent, fixed‑term (allowed if justified), temporary, apprenticeship.
*   Probationary Periods: 3 months (general), 6 months (skilled/management), 12 months (highly specialized).
*   Mandatory Clauses: wages, hours, leave, termination conditions.
*   Language Requirement: Spanish mandatory.
*   Written Contracts: Required for fixed‑term and apprenticeship contracts.

**3\. Working Hours & Overtime**

*   Standard Hours: 8 hours/day, 48 hours/week.
*   Overtime Rules: Paid at _1.25×_ hourly wage (first 2 hours), _1.35×_ thereafter.
*   Rest Periods: Weekly rest day (Sunday).

**4\. Leave & Holidays**

*   Annual Leave: 30 calendar days/year (after 1 year of service).
*   Public Holidays: ~12 annually (including Independence Day, Christmas, New Year, Easter).
*   Sick Leave: First 20 days paid by employer, thereafter by social security (EsSalud).
*   Maternity Leave: 98 days (paid, 49 before and 49 after birth).
*   Paternity Leave: 10 days (paid).

**5\. Compensation & Benefits**

*   Minimum Wage: Adjusted periodically (~S/1,025/month in 2025).
*   Mandatory Contributions:
    *   Pension fund (AFP or ONP): employee ~10%.
    *   Health insurance (EsSalud): employer ~9%.
    *   Unemployment insurance: included in severance rules.
*   Bonus/13th Month Pay: Mandatory July and December bonuses (equivalent to one month’s salary each).
*   Profit Sharing: Mandatory for companies with >20 employees (percentage varies by sector).

**6\. Termination & Severance**

*   Notice Periods: 30 days (unless payment in lieu).
*   Severance Pay:
    *   1.5 monthly salaries per year of service (capped at 12 months).
*   Exemptions: No severance if dismissed for misconduct.

**7\. Employee Obligations**

*   Must comply with company rules and statutory obligations.
*   Must give contractual notice for resignation (usually 30 days).
*   Employees must contribute to pension and health systems.

**8\. Compliance & Enforcement**

*   Labour Inspectorate (SUNAFIL): Oversees compliance.
*   Labour Courts: Handle disputes.
*   CBAs: Binding and enforceable across industries.

**9\. References**

*   Ministry of Labour and Employment Promotion (MTPE) – official portal.
