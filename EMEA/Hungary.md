**Country Name: Hungary**

**🇭🇺 Hungary – HR & Labor Law Guide (2026)**

**1\. Country Overview**

*   Primary Laws:
    *   _Labour Code (Act I of 2012)_ – core employment framework.
    *   _Social Security Act_.
    *   _Civil Code_ (general contractual obligations).
*   Regulatory Authority: Ministry of Innovation and Technology (Labour Department).
*   Applicability: Covers all employees; collective bargaining agreements (CBAs) supplement statutory rules.

**2\. Employment Contracts**

*   Types of Employment:
    *   Permanent (open‑ended).
    *   Fixed‑term (maximum 5 years).
    *   Part‑time.
    *   Temporary/agency work.
*   Probationary Periods: Up to 3 months (extendable to 6 months by collective agreement).
*   Mandatory Clauses: job description, wages, hours, leave, termination conditions.
*   Language Requirement: Hungarian is mandatory; bilingual contracts allowed but Hungarian version prevails.

**3\. Working Hours & Overtime**

*   Standard Hours: 40 hours/week (8 hours/day).
*   Overtime Rules:
    *   _1.5×_ hourly wage → overtime.
    *   _2×_ hourly wage → work on Sundays/public holidays.
*   Rest Periods: 11 hours daily rest; 48 hours weekly rest.

**4\. Leave & Holidays**

*   Annual Leave:
    *   20 working days/year minimum.
    *   Increases with age (up to 30 days).
*   Public Holidays: 11 annually (e.g., National Day, Easter, Christmas).
*   Sick Leave: Paid; employer covers 70% of wage for first 15 days, thereafter social security.
*   Maternity Leave: 24 weeks (paid).
*   Paternity Leave: 10 working days (paid).
*   Parental Leave: Up to 3 years (job protected, allowance available).

**5\. Compensation & Benefits**

*   Minimum Wage: Adjusted annually (~HUF 350,000/month in 2025).
*   Overtime Pay: As above.
*   Mandatory Contributions:
    *   Social security (employer ~13%, employee ~18.5%).
    *   Health, pension, unemployment.
*   Bonus/13th Month Pay: Not statutory, but common in some industries.

**6\. Termination & Severance**

*   Notice Periods:
    *   30 days minimum.
    *   Can extend up to 6 months depending on tenure.
*   Severance Pay:
    *   1 month’s wages (≥3 years service).
    *   Scales up to 6 months (≥25 years service).
*   Exemptions: No severance if dismissed for misconduct.

**7\. Employee Obligations**

*   Must comply with company rules and statutory obligations.
*   Must give contractual notice for resignation.
*   Misconduct (fraud, theft, violence) can justify dismissal without severance.
*   Employees must contribute to social security.

**8\. Compliance & Enforcement**

*   Labour Inspectorate (Hungarian Labour Authority): Enforces compliance with labour law.
*   Penalties: Fines for breaches of working time, wage, and safety rules.
*   Dispute Resolution: Labour courts handle disputes.
