**Country Name: Turkey**

**🇹🇷 Turkey – HR & Labour Law Guide (2026)**

**1\. Country Overview**

*   Primary Laws:
    *   _Labour Law No. 4857 (2003)_ – contracts, working hours, termination.
    *   _Social Insurance and General Health Insurance Law No. 5510_.
    *   _Occupational Health and Safety Law No. 6331_.
*   Regulatory Authority: Ministry of Labour and Social Security.
*   Applicability: Covers all employees; CBAs supplement statutory rules.

**2\. Employment Contracts**

*   Types of Employment:
    *   Permanent (open‑ended).
    *   Fixed‑term (allowed if justified).
    *   Part‑time.
    *   Temporary/seasonal work.
*   Probationary Periods: Max 2 months (extendable to 4 months via CBAs).
*   Mandatory Clauses: wages, hours, leave, termination conditions.
*   Language Requirement: Turkish is mandatory; bilingual contracts allowed but Turkish prevails.

**3\. Working Hours & Overtime**

*   Standard Hours: 45 hours/week (distributed over 6 days).
*   Overtime Rules:
    *   _1.5×_ hourly wage → overtime weekdays.
    *   _2×_ hourly wage → weekends/public holidays.
*   Rest Periods: 11 hours daily rest; 24 hours weekly rest.

**4\. Leave & Holidays**

*   Annual Leave (based on tenure):
    *   14 days (1–5 years service).
    *   20 days (5–15 years).
    *   26 days (≥15 years).
*   Public Holidays: 14 annually (e.g., Republic Day, National Sovereignty Day, Eid al‑Fitr, Eid al‑Adha).
*   Sick Leave: Paid via social insurance; employer covers initial period.
*   Maternity Leave: 16 weeks (8 before, 8 after birth, paid).
*   Paternity Leave: 5 days (paid).
*   Parental Leave: Up to 6 months unpaid (job protected).

**5\. Compensation & Benefits**

*   Minimum Wage: Adjusted annually (~TRY 17,002/month gross in 2025).
*   Mandatory Contributions:
    *   Social security (employer ~22.5%, employee ~14%).
    *   Covers pension, health, unemployment, accident insurance.
*   Bonus/13th Month Pay: Not statutory, but common under CBAs.

**6\. Termination & Severance**

*   Notice Periods (based on tenure):
    *   2 weeks (< 6 months service)
    *   4 weeks (6–18 months).
    *   6 weeks (18–36 months).
    *   8 weeks (≥3 years).
*   Severance Pay:
    *   30 days’ wages per year of service (capped at statutory ceiling).
*   Exemptions: No severance if dismissed for misconduct.

**7\. Employee Obligations**

*   Must comply with company rules and statutory obligations.
*   Must give contractual notice for resignation.
*   Employees must contribute to social security.

**8\. Compliance & Enforcement**

*   Labour Inspectorate (Ministry of Labour and Social Security): Enforces compliance.
*   Labour Courts: Handle disputes.
*   CBAs: Binding and enforceable across industries.
