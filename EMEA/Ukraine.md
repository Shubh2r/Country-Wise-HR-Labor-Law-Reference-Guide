**Country Name: Ukraine**

**🇺🇦 Ukraine – HR & Labour Law Guide (2026)**

**1\. Country Overview**

*   Primary Laws:
    *   _Labour Code of Ukraine (1971, with amendments)_ – contracts, working hours, termination.
    *   _Law on Remuneration of Labour_.
    *   _Law on Occupational Safety and Health_.
*   Regulatory Authority: Ministry of Social Policy of Ukraine.
*   Applicability: Covers all employees; CBAs supplement statutory rules.

**2\. Employment Contracts**

*   Types of Employment:
    *   Permanent (open‑ended).
    *   Fixed‑term (allowed if justified).
    *   Part‑time.
    *   Temporary/seasonal work.
*   Probationary Periods: Max 3 months (6 months for managerial roles).
*   Mandatory Clauses: wages, hours, leave, termination conditions.
*   Language Requirement: Ukrainian is mandatory; bilingual contracts allowed but Ukrainian prevails.

**3\. Working Hours & Overtime**

*   Standard Hours: 40 hours/week.
*   Overtime Rules:
    *   _2×_ hourly wage → overtime hours.
    *   Overtime capped at 120 hours/year.
*   Rest Periods: 12 hours daily rest; 42 hours weekly rest.

**4\. Leave & Holidays**

*   Annual Leave:
    *   24 calendar days/year minimum.
*   Public Holidays: 11 annually (e.g., Independence Day, Constitution Day, Orthodox Christmas, Easter).
*   Sick Leave: Paid via social insurance; employer covers initial period.
*   Maternity Leave: 126 days (70 before, 56 after birth, paid at 100%).
*   Paternity Leave: 14 days (paid).
*   Parental Leave: Up to 3 years unpaid (job protected).

**5\. Compensation & Benefits**

*   Minimum Wage: Adjusted annually (~UAH 8,000/month in 2025).
*   Mandatory Contributions:
    *   Unified Social Contribution (employer ~22%).
    *   Covers pension, health, unemployment, accident insurance.
*   Bonus/13th Month Pay: Not statutory, but common under CBAs.

**6\. Termination & Severance**

*   Notice Periods:
    *   2 weeks (employee resignation).
    *   1 month (employer termination, depending on grounds).
*   Severance Pay:
    *   1 month’s average wage (redundancy).
*   Exemptions: No severance if dismissed for misconduct.

**7\. Employee Obligations**

*   Must comply with company rules and statutory obligations.
*   Must give contractual notice for resignation.
*   Employees must contribute to social insurance.

**8\. Compliance & Enforcement**

*   Labour Inspectorate (State Labour Service of Ukraine): Enforces compliance.
*   Labour Courts: Handle disputes.
*   CBAs: Binding and enforceable across industries.
