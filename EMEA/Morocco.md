**Country Name: Morocco**

**🇲🇦 Morocco – HR & Labor Law Guide (2026)**

**1\. Country Overview**

*   Primary Laws:
    *   _Moroccan Labour Code (2004)_ – core employment framework.
    *   _Social Security Code_.
    *   _Collective Bargaining Agreements (CBAs)_.
*   Regulatory Authority: Ministry of Economic Inclusion, Small Business, Employment and Skills.
*   Applicability: Covers all employees; CBAs supplement statutory rules.

**2\. Employment Contracts**

*   Types of Employment:
    *   Permanent (open‑ended).
    *   Fixed‑term (max 2 years, renewable once).
    *   Part‑time.
    *   Temporary/seasonal.
*   Probationary Periods:
    *   3 months (executives).
    *   1.5 months (white‑collar).
    *   15 days (blue‑collar).
*   Mandatory Clauses: wages, hours, leave, termination conditions.
*   Language Requirement: Arabic or French; bilingual contracts allowed.

**3\. Working Hours & Overtime**

*   Standard Hours: 44 hours/week (8 hours/day).
*   Overtime Rules:
    *   _1.25×_ hourly wage → overtime weekdays.
    *   _1.5×_ hourly wage → overtime nights/weekends.
    *   _2×_ hourly wage → public holidays.
*   Rest Periods: 24 hours weekly rest (Sunday common).

**4\. Leave & Holidays**

*   Annual Leave:
    *   18 working days/year minimum (after 1 year service).
    *   Increases with tenure (up to 22 days).
*   Public Holidays: 13 annually (religious + national).
*   Sick Leave: Paid; employer covers part, social security covers remainder.
*   Maternity Leave: 14 weeks (paid).
*   Paternity Leave: 3 days (paid).
*   Parental Leave: Job protected; adoption leave available.

**5\. Compensation & Benefits**

*   Minimum Wage (SMIG): Adjusted annually (~MAD 3,700/month in 2025 for private sector).
*   Overtime Pay: As above.
*   Mandatory Contributions:
    *   CNSS (social security): employer ~16.5%, employee ~6.7%.
    *   Covers pension, health, family allowances.
*   Bonus/13th Month Pay: Not statutory, but common under CBAs.

**6\. Termination & Severance**

*   Notice Periods:
    *   1 month (
    *   2 months (≥1 year service).
*   Severance Pay:
    *   96 hours’ wages (1–5 years service).
    *   144 hours’ wages (5–10 years).
    *   192 hours’ wages (10–15 years).
    *   240 hours’ wages (≥15 years).
*   Exemptions: No severance if dismissed for misconduct.

**7\. Employee Obligations**

*   Must comply with company rules and statutory obligations.
*   Must give contractual notice for resignation.
*   Misconduct (fraud, theft, violence) can justify dismissal without severance.
*   Employees must contribute to CNSS.

**8\. Compliance & Enforcement**

*   Labour Inspectorate (Ministry of Labour): Enforces compliance with labour law.
*   Labour Courts: Handle disputes.
*   CBAs: Binding and enforceable across industries.

*   **Collective Bargaining Agreements (CBAs)**: Define sector‑specific wages, bonuses, and entitlements.
*   **Social Security (CNSS)**: Covers pension, health, family allowances.
*   **Language Requirement**: Contracts must be in Arabic or French; bilingual versions allowed.
*   **Recent Developments**:
