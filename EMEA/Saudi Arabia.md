**Country Name: Saudi Arabia**

**🇸🇦 Saudi Arabia – HR & Labor Law Guide (2026)**

**1\. Country Overview**

*   Primary Laws:
    *   _Saudi Labour Law (2005, amended 2019)_ – core employment framework.
    *   _Social Insurance Law_.
    *   _Occupational Safety and Health Regulations_.
*   Regulatory Authority: Ministry of Human Resources and Social Development (MHRSD).
*   Applicability: Covers Saudi nationals and expatriates; CBAs are rare but exist in some sectors.

**2\. Employment Contracts**

*   Types of Employment:
    *   Permanent (open‑ended).
    *   Fixed‑term (common for expatriates).
    *   Part‑time.
*   Probationary Periods: Up to 90 days (extendable to 180 days with agreement).
*   Mandatory Clauses: wages, hours, leave, termination conditions.
*   Language Requirement: Arabic is mandatory; bilingual contracts allowed but Arabic prevails.

**3\. Working Hours & Overtime**

*   Standard Hours: 48 hours/week (8 hours/day).
*   Ramadan Hours: 36 hours/week (6 hours/day) for Muslim employees.
*   Overtime Rules:
    *   _1.5×_ hourly wage → overtime weekdays.
    *   _2×_ hourly wage → weekends/public holidays.
*   Rest Periods: 24 hours weekly rest (Friday common).

**4\. Leave & Holidays**

*   Annual Leave:
    *   21 days/year minimum (after 1 year service).
    *   30 days/year (after 5 years service).
*   Public Holidays: 10–12 annually (Eid al‑Fitr, Eid al‑Adha, National Day).
*   Sick Leave: 30 days full pay + 60 days three‑quarters pay per year.
*   Maternity Leave: 10 weeks (paid).
*   Paternity Leave: 3 days (paid).
*   Hajj Leave: 10–15 days (once during employment, for Muslim employees).

**5\. Compensation & Benefits**

*   Minimum Wage: ~SAR 4,000/month for Saudi nationals (2025).
*   Overtime Pay: As above.
*   Mandatory Contributions:
    *   General Organization for Social Insurance (GOSI): employer ~12%, employee ~10%.
    *   Covers pension, health, unemployment, accident insurance.
*   Bonus/13th Month Pay: Not statutory, but common in some industries.

**6\. Termination & Severance**

*   Notice Periods:
    *   60 days (monthly contracts).
    *   30 days (daily/weekly contracts).
*   Severance Pay:
    *   Half month’s wages per year of service (first 5 years).
    *   1 month’s wages per year of service (after 5 years).
*   Exemptions: No severance if dismissed for misconduct.

**7\. Employee Obligations**

*   Must comply with company rules and statutory obligations.
*   Must give contractual notice for resignation.
*   Misconduct (fraud, theft, violence) can justify dismissal without severance.
*   Employees must contribute to GOSI.

**8\. Compliance & Enforcement**

*   Labour Inspectorate (MHRSD): Enforces compliance with labour law.
*   Labour Courts: Handle disputes.
*   CBAs: Rare, but binding where they exist.
