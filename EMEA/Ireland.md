**Country Name: Ireland**

**🇮🇪 Ireland – HR & Labor Law Guide (2026)**

**1\. Country Overview**

*   Primary Laws:
    *   _Employment Equality Acts (1998–2015)_.
    *   _Organisation of Working Time Act (1997)_.
    *   _Terms of Employment (Information) Act (1994–2014)_.
    *   _Unfair Dismissals Acts (1977–2015)_.
    *   _Redundancy Payments Acts_.
*   Regulatory Authority: Workplace Relations Commission (WRC).
*   Applicability: Covers all employees; collective agreements supplement statutory rules.

**2\. Employment Contracts**

*   Types of Employment:
    *   Permanent (open‑ended).
    *   Fixed‑term.
    *   Part‑time.
    *   Agency/temporary.
*   Probationary Periods: Typically 6 months, extendable to 11 months.
*   Mandatory Clauses: job description, wages, hours, leave, termination conditions.
*   Language Requirement: English or Irish; must be clear and comprehensible.

**3\. Working Hours & Overtime**

*   Standard Hours: 39–40 hours/week (average).
*   Overtime Rules:
    *   No statutory overtime rate; determined by contract or collective agreement.
*   Rest Periods: 11 hours daily rest; 24 hours weekly rest.

**4\. Leave & Holidays**

*   Annual Leave (Organisation of Working Time Act):
    *   4 weeks/year minimum.
*   Public Holidays: 9 annually (e.g., St. Patrick’s Day, Christmas).
*   Sick Leave: Paid sick leave introduced in 2022; entitlement increasing gradually (10 days by 2026).
*   Maternity Leave: 26 weeks (paid by social insurance), plus 16 weeks unpaid.
*   Paternity Leave: 2 weeks (paid).
*   Parental Leave: 26 weeks per parent (unpaid, job protected).
*   Parent’s Leave: 9 weeks (paid, introduced in recent reforms).

**5\. Compensation & Benefits**

*   Minimum Wage: Adjusted annually (~€12.70/hour in 2025).
*   Overtime Pay: As per contract/collective agreement.
*   Mandatory Contributions:
    *   Pay Related Social Insurance (PRSI): employer ~11.05%, employee ~4%.
    *   Universal Social Charge (USC).
*   Bonus/13th Month Pay: Not statutory, but common in some industries.

**6\. Termination & Severance**

*   Notice Periods (Minimum Notice & Terms of Employment Act):
    *   1 week (
    *   Scales up to 8 weeks (≥15 years service).
*   Severance Pay (Redundancy Payments Acts):
    *   2 weeks’ pay per year of service + bonus week.
    *   Capped at €600/week.
*   Exemptions: No severance if dismissed for misconduct.

**7\. Employee Obligations**

*   Must comply with company rules and statutory obligations.
*   Must give contractual notice for resignation.
*   Misconduct (fraud, theft, violence) can justify dismissal without severance.
*   Employees must contribute to PRSI and USC.

**8\. Compliance & Enforcement**

*   Workplace Relations Commission (WRC): Enforces compliance with labour law.
*   Labour Court: Handles disputes and appeals.
*   Penalties: Fines for breaches of working time, wage, and safety rules.
