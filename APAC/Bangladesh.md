**Country Name: Bangladesh**

**🇧🇩 Bangladesh – HR & Labour Law Guide (2026)**

**1\. Country Overview**

*   Primary Laws:
    *   _Bangladesh Labour Act, 2006_ (amended 2013, 2018, 2022) – contracts, rights, termination.
    *   _Bangladesh Labour Rules, 2015_.
    *   _Factories Act, 1965_ – workplace safety.
*   Regulatory Authority: Ministry of Labour and Employment.
*   Applicability: Covers all employees; CBAs supplement statutory rules.
*   Recent Developments (2022–25):
*   Amendments to strengthen maternity benefits, union rights, and workplace safety.
*   Ongoing reforms in minimum wage boards for garment and industrial sectors.

**2\. Employment Contracts**

*   Types of Employment:
    *   Permanent (indefinite).
    *   Fixed‑term (allowed if justified).
    *   Temporary/seasonal work.
    *   Apprenticeship contracts.
*   Probationary Periods:
    *   6 months (clerical).
    *   3 months (other categories).
*   Mandatory Clauses: wages, hours, leave, termination conditions.
*   Language Requirement: Bengali mandatory.

**3\. Working Hours & Overtime**

*   Standard Hours: 8 hours/day, 48 hours/week.
*   Overtime Rules:
    *   _2×_ hourly wage → overtime.
*   Rest Periods: 1 day weekly rest.

**4\. Leave & Holidays**

*   Annual Leave:
    *   1 day for every 18 days worked (≈14–20 days/year depending on category).
*   Public Holidays: 11 annually (e.g., Independence Day, Victory Day, Eid, Christmas).
*   Sick Leave: 14 days/year (paid).
*   Maternity Leave: 16 weeks (paid, up to 2 surviving children).
*   Paternity Leave: Not statutory, but some employers provide 5–10 days.

**5\. Compensation & Benefits**

*   Minimum Wage: Sector‑specific; garment sector ~BDT 8,000/month (2023), under review for 2026.
*   Mandatory Contributions:
    *   Provident fund (optional, but common in large companies).
    *   Workers’ welfare fund contributions.
*   Bonus/13th Month Pay: Two festival bonuses (mandatory, usually equivalent to basic salary).

**6\. Termination & Severance**

*   Notice Periods:
    *   120 days (permanent workers).
    *   60 days (temporary workers).
*   Severance Pay:
    *   30 days’ wages per year of service (redundancy/unfair dismissal).
*   Exemptions: No severance if dismissed for misconduct.

**7\. Employee Obligations**

*   Must comply with company rules and statutory obligations.
*   Must give contractual notice for resignation (usually 30–60 days).
*   Employees must contribute to provident fund if applicable.

**8\. Compliance & Enforcement**

*   Labour Inspectorate: Oversees compliance.
*   Labour Courts: Handle disputes.
*   CBAs: Binding and enforceable across industries.
